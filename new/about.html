Introduction to Agroforestry

Agroforestry is a land use management system that integrates trees and shrubs into agricultural landscapes. It is designed to create more sustainable and productive farming systems.

  • Definition and Principles: Agroforestry involves growing trees and crops together to enhance biodiversity and ecosystem services.
  • Benefits and Importance: It provides multiple benefits including improved soil health, increased biodiversity, and enhanced water management.
  • Types of Agroforestry Systems: Includes alley cropping, silvopasture, and forest farming.
