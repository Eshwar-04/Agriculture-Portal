Training and Workshops

Agroforestry Basics Workshop

This workshop covers the fundamental concepts of agroforestry, including the integration of trees and shrubs into agricultural landscapes to enhance productivity and sustainability.

  • Topics Covered: Introduction to Agroforestry, Benefits, Design Principles.
  • Duration: 2 Days
  • Location: Local Agricultural Extension Office

Advanced Agroforestry Techniques

This training focuses on advanced techniques and practices in agroforestry, including soil management, pest control, and integration with modern farming practices.

  • Topics Covered: Soil Health, Pest Management, Advanced Design.
  • Duration: 3 Days
  • Location: Regional Agricultural Research Center

Sustainable Agroforestry Practices

This program highlights sustainable practices within agroforestry systems, aiming to improve environmental health and ensure long-term viability of farming systems.

  • Topics Covered: Sustainable Practices, Environmental Impact, Long-term Planning.
  • Duration: 1 Week
  • Location: Community Training Center

Organic Farming and Agroforestry Integration

This workshop explores how organic farming methods can be integrated with agroforestry to enhance soil health and crop yields while reducing chemical inputs.

  • Topics Covered: Organic Farming Techniques, Agroforestry Integration, Soil Fertility.
  • Duration: 4 Days
  • Location: Organic Farming Institute

Tree Crop Management Training

This training focuses on the management of tree crops within agroforestry systems, including pruning, pest control, and harvesting techniques.

  • Topics Covered: Tree Crop Pruning, Pest Management, Harvesting Techniques.
  • Duration: 3 Days
  • Location: Local Forestry Office

Community Agroforestry Projects

This program provides training on implementing agroforestry projects within communities, focusing on collaboration, project planning, and management.

  • Topics Covered: Community Involvement, Project Planning, Management Strategies.
  • Duration: 5 Days
  • Location: Community Development Center
